Our Mission
Why NariShakti?

In India, millions of women lack access to quality skill training due to financial, social, and geographic barriers. NariShakti breaks these barriers by bringing free, certified training directly to every woman's device.

Built on AWS Amplify, our platform ensures 99.9% uptime, fast loading in low-bandwidth areas, and secure data — so no woman is left behind.

Our courses are developed by industry experts and NGO partners, designed to lead to real employment, self-employment, or higher education opportunities.
